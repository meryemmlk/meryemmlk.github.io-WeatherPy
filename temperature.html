Average Temperature

In this graph we have the temperature plotted against latitude. The plot clearly shows that the temperature increses as the latitude comes closer to 0. Which means temperature increases as we come closer to the equator either from south or north. Around -20 and 20 degrees latitude we observe the maximum temperature.
